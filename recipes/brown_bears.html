Brown Bears

Simple Brown Bear Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated biscuit dough

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Mix cinnamon and sugar together in a bowl. Pour melted butter into another bowl
  3. Separate biscuits and form each piece of dough into a rope 4 to 5 inches long. Wrap the dough pieces around sticks
  4. Hold sticks over campfire and slowly turn until the biscuit dough is browned and set, 8 to 10 minutes
  5. Dip biscuits into melted butter and then into cinnamon sugar. Eat biscuits from sticks
  6. Enjoy!
